This video is part 1 of the live course series at MIT "Reparations for Slavery and Colonization: Contemporary Movements for Justice" during Fall 2021. 

This lecture by Liliane Umubyeyi is an introduction to how reparations are being pursued by different actors around the world, for different purposes and motivations. Specifically, this lectures gives a legal overview to how different groups have sought reparations in the courts from different countries.

Liliane Umubyeyi is the co-funder and Executive Director of African Futures Lab.
