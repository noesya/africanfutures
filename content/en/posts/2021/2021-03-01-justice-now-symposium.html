Organized in March 2021, the Justice Now? symposium - external link gathered speakers in areas such as transitional justice, racial justice, museum, legal, colonial and Decolonial studies discussed experiences and shared strategies in the development of anti-racist and decolonial movements. The symposium was convened by the Anthropology department at MIT - external link in collaboration with international human rights NGO Avocats Sans Frontières - external link.
