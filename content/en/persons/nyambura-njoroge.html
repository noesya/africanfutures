Nyambura Njoroge, a Kenyan citizen, is currently pursuing a Master's Degree in Sustainable Development at KU Leuven, Belgium. With an unwavering commitment to environmental issues and sustainability, she is passionate about researching, analyzing and implementing projects that advocate for positive change within her community.

She has honed her skills working with organizations such as the Africa Waste Environment Management Center (AWEMAC) and the Wildlife Clubs of Kenya (WCK).

As an intern at AfaLab, Nyambura works with the team on a variety of projects, using her expertise to lead impactful initiatives and promote sustainable development.
