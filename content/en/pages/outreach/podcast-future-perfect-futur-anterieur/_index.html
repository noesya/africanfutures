Future Perfect | Futur Antérieur highlights individual and collective actions across Europe and Africa aimed at defending racial equality and justice. Our guests - scholars, activists, artists - share their actions with us, highlighting both the forms that historical and contemporary racial violence takes in their context and examples of possible reforms and mobilisations. Through their experiences, we draw the contours of racial justice efforts today.
