African Futures Lab increases the understanding of the ways racial injustices intersect with socio-economic, environmental and gender inequalities across Africa and Europe through the production of research reports and policy papers.Â 

Our research reports and policy papers are action-oriented: Ideas and knowledge inform public discussions and guide decision-makers in their pursuit of reform.

Recognising that knowledge production is inherently political, our research methodology centres on historically marginalised voices who have been disenfranchised based on race, nationality, gender, religion, ability, or other factors.

Our research methodology takes a cross-disciplinary approach: We value a wide range of expertise, and we invite as many different voices to the table as possible. We believe that expanding the proverbial table strengthens the work.
