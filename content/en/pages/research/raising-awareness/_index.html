  • We are action-oriented: Ideas and knowledge developed in the Lab can inform public discussions and guide decision-makers in their efforts to undertake reform.
  • Recognizing that the production of knowledge is profoundly political, we center historically marginalized voices, based on race, nationality, gender, religion, ability, or other factors.
  • We take a cross-disciplinary approach: We value a wide range of expertise and we invite as many different voices to the table as possible. We believe that expanding the proverbial table strengthens the work.
