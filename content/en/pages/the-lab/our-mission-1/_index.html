Until now, state policies and civil society programs to combat racial injustices have focused on inequalities in specific nations without looking at their global manifestation. 

notes: >- alt: >- credit: >- - kind: heading title: >- position: 1 rank: 2 - kind: block template: chapter title: >- ranks: self: false data: layout: no_background text: >-

Racial domination is evident in global relations between Europe and Africa, particularly in social, economic, migratory, and climatic issues. The history of colonization and its contemporary consequences have resulted in inequalities in development between the two continents that have a racial dimension, which is often overlooked.

This racial dimension is invisible and obscured by policies and programs of action aimed at reducing development inequalities between the two continents.

notes: >- alt: >- credit: >- - kind: block template: chapter title: >- ranks: self: false data: layout: accent_background text: >-

African Futures Lab’s mission is to raise awareness of global racial injustices across Africa and Europe and empower civil society actors, as well as public and private entities, to demand justice and achieve reform.
