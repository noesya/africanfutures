AfaLab tackles the lack of accountability for global racial injustices across Europe and Africa:

  1. The denial of historical and contemporary racial violence by former colonial and slaveholding states
  2. The absence (or insufficiency) of transnational mobilization to challenge global racial injustices.

To tackle this lack of accountability, we deploys two strategies:

  1. Investigating historical and contemporary global racial injustices and potential solutions through field surveys, analysis of statistical information, and archival research. We publish the results of our  research in in-depth research reports. 
  2. Advocating policy changes and structural reforms through transnational coalitions across Europe and Africa.
