Job Title: Communication Officer 

Location: East Africa (preferably in Nairobi or with the possibility to travel regularly in Nairobi)

Length of Contract: 3 years 

Preferred Start Date: As soon as possible 

Application Deadline: January 12, 2025

notes: >- alt: >- credit: >- - kind: block template: title title: >- About the Position: Communication Officer slug: >- about-the-position-communication-officer ranks: base: 2 self: 2 top: active: true title: value: >- About the Position: Communication Officer heading: 2 data: layout: classic - kind: block template: chapter title: >- slug: >- ranks: base: 3 top: active: false data: layout: no_background text: >-

The Communication Officer will play a pivotal role in amplifying AfaLab's visibility and advancing its mission through impactful communication strategies. This role offers an exciting opportunity to implement a communication strategy designed to raise awareness about AfaLab and its work, particularly as 2025 has been named the year for “Justice for Africans and People of African Descent Through Reparations”, making it a critical year for racial justice advocacy.

Core Responsibilities:

I)   Analytical Abilities 

  •   Review research reports and distilling key insights into accessible, visually appealing and impactful messaging.
  • Track traditional and social media to identify relevant articles, events, or reports, analyse their impact on the organization, and inform strategy.

II)   Content Creation & Graphic Design 

  • Develop and implement an editorial calendar for the organization's regular communications, including managing and updating the organization's website and social media platforms (LinkedIn, Instagram, Newsletter).
  • Plan and ensure the communication coverage of the organization’s events, reports/projects in collaboration with the team.
  • Measure, analyse and report on effectiveness of social media content and strategy to improve the organization social media presence and engagement. 
  • Create cross-platform content outputs which achieve the organization’s advocacy and campaign's goals.

III)  Media Engagement & Partnerships

  •  Develop and maintain strong relationships with journalists to increase media coverage of AfaLab’s activities and advocacy efforts.
  • Develop and update a press/media database.
  • Draft press releases and manage a press rollout from start to finish.

  •  Education: Master degree or equivalent in communications, journalism, political science, or a related area.
  •  Experience: 3 to 5 years of professional experience in communications, journalism, or a related field.
  •  Languages: Excellent command of English (C1 spoken and written) with a good command of French (writing and speaking proficiency required).
  •  Knowledge: Familiarity with racial justice topics and current global advocacy trends.

  Technical Skills:

  • Exceptional writing skills with the ability to craft clear and impactful content.
  • Strong sense of aesthetics and design.
  • Proficiency with software such as Microsoft Word, Excel, Adobe Creative Suite(InDesign, Photoshop, Illustrator) and Canva.
  • Basic video and photo editing skills. 

  Organizational Competencies:

  • Effectively manage multiple projects and deadlines in a dynamic, fast-paced environment.
  • Adapt to shifting priorities and organizational needs with strong self-organization skills
  • Ability to work independently in remote locations while collaborating in occasional team meetings.
  • Ability to build and maintain relationships with diverse partners.
  • Technical Skills: Proficiency in Microsoft Word, Excel, and Google Workspace.
  • Travel Requirements: Willing to attend events and meetings, with travel up to 20%. 

What We Are Looking For:

We seek a colleague who:

  • Thrives in a small, dynamic team environment, embracing adaptability and collaboration.
  • Demonstrates exceptional interpersonal skills and the ability to foster collaboration.
  • Embodies a commitment to justice, integrity, creativity, reliability, and optimism, reflecting core organizational values.
  • Is passionate about racial justice and dedicated to advancing AfaLab’s mission.

Job Title: Climate Justice Research & Policy Adviser

Location: East Africa based position (preferably in Nairobi or with the possibility to travel regularly in Nairobi)

Length of Contract: 3 years

Preferred Start Date: As soon as possible

Deadline: January 12, 2025

The Climate Justice Research and Policy Adviser (CRPA) is responsible for overseeing the development of African Futures Lab's strategy and action plan for its climate justice & reparations agenda. More specifically, the CRPA will be responsible for defining and coordinating a strategic research agenda that will feed the advocacy activities. Identifying key partners (civil society, media, etc.) and strategic advocacy areas in Africa and globally will be the central pillar of this position.

Core Responsibilities:

I) Strategy Development: Climate Justice and Reparations

  • Expand on AfaLab's work, by formulating a comprehensive climate reparations strategy and action plan to be deployed across Africa and on the global scale.

  • Develop and update work plans and provide recommendations for priority files and opportunities.

  • Draft policy papers by developing arguments in relation to the desired policy change.

II) Research and Analysis

  • Design, coordinate, and supervise in depth research reports that apply to the assigned policy areas.

  • Contribute to the publication process of research reports.

  • Monitor and analyse developments in external trends (policies, programs, research)

    with actual or potential impact on matters related to climate justice and reparations

III) Influencing Public Debates on Climate Justice and Reparations

  • Utilize media production tools, such as podcasts and videos, to effectively disseminate knowledge.

  • Design and coordinate activities and events that feed into supporting and achieving the priorities and goals of the assigned policy area.

  • Represent AfaLab relevant academic, policy, and activist forums to build support and foster collaboration.

  • Draft and publish op-eds to engage broader audiences and influence public discourse.
