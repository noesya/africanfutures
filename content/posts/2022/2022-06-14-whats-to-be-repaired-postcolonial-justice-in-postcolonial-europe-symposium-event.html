The European launch of the AfaLab will be happening at the 'What's to be repaired? - Postcolonial Justice in Postcolonial Europe.' This event will be taking place during the international symposium at Utrecht University on June 23rd. Co-Directors Amah Edoh and Liliane Umubyeyi will speak on the need to build a reparative justice agenda in Europe. 

When: 23rd of June from 16:00 to 17:30.

Location: Casco Art Institue  - Lange Nieuwstraat 7,  3512PA Utrecht. 

The symposium will also host Mame-Fatou Niang, an AfaLab Fellow. As part of the Lecture series Race et culture, she will be presenting "French, but not (Q) White?: Expanding Frenchness for the 21st Century."

For the daily programme, locations, and further updates, please check the event website. 

For registration, please send an email to this address: whatstoberepaired2022@gmail.com
