Some states in the Global North, such as the United States or Belgium, have recently used transitional justice mechanisms to deal with their colonial or slavery past. Based on her experience on the African continent, Annah Moyo presents in this video the opportunities and constraints offered by transitional justice processes to address the crimes of colonization and slavery.
